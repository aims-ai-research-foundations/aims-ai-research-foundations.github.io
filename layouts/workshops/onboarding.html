{{ partial "section-eyebrow.html" (dict "eyebrow" "The virtual pre-work period" "title" "What the pre-work involves") }}

The virtual pre-work prepares you for a successful workshop. During this phase, you complete at least the first four AI Research Foundations courses on Google Skills.

By completing the pre-work online, everyone arrives at the in-person workshop with a shared technical foundation. This lets the week focus on deeper learning, teaching practice, and adapting the curriculum for your own students.

During the pre-work period, you will:

  • Complete the AI Research Foundations courses 01 to 04 on Google Skills
  • Join the Discord community
  • Attend the weekly live sessions
  • Engage with your cohort and the programme facilitators
  • Arrive fully prepared for the workshop
