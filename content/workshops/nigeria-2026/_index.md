The Nigeria Lecturer Workshop is the first national workshop of the programme. Lecturers and teaching assistants from participating Nigerian universities come together for an intensive, fully funded week at the University of Lagos, hosted by the FATE Foundation.

Both cohorts are onboarded together during the pre-work period, then attend the in-person week in two groups across August. By the end of the week, participants are ready to deliver the AI Research Foundations curriculum to their own students, adapted for their context.
